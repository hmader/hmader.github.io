Election Coverage

As part of their coverage of the 2014 U.S. Midterm elections, Univision Noticias partnered with the University of Miami School of Communications to create data visualizations that would be published on UnivisionNoticias.com, their political blog Pol16, and its official Twitter account. I participated as a member of this team of undergraduate and graduate students, working individually and in groups to generate clear and accurate visualizations of incoming data to be published in real-time on UnivisionNoticias.com, "Pol16," Univision's political blog, and Twitter.

Our team worked late into the night from Univision's building in Miami, FL as votes came in from around the country. The experience was stressful at times - tiring too, as the hours ticked by - but mostly exciting and an incredible opportunity to work in a newsroom.
